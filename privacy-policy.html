PRIVACY POLICY MODEL FOR IREPORT STANDARD EDITION MOBILE APPLICATION

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application iReport Standard Edition (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by iReportSource, Inc. The Application allows the user to collect information and generate reports about various business related incidents, such as injuries / fatalities of workers, auto accidents, near misses, etc.

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you use the application.

When you use the Application, you generally provide the following information about yourself and/or others that are relevant to the incident reporting process, including but not limited to:

ꞏ first and last name

ꞏ employee ID

ꞏ last four digits of the social security number

ꞏ mailing address

ꞏ phone number

ꞏ email address

ꞏ birthdate and age

ꞏ medications being used

ꞏ medical history / conditions

ꞏ descriptions of behavior, including possible criminal behavior

ꞏ physcian name and contact information

ꞏ vehicle information, includind license plates

ꞏ photographs of individuals and specific body parts

ꞏ audio recordings of individuals

ꞏ electronic signatures

 

We may also use the information you provided us to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

 

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

 

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

When you utilize the mobile application, we may use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current location in order to specifically identify the location at which a specific instance occured, especially if the incident occurred outside of a specific address.

If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you should turn off the location services for the mobile application located in your mobile phone settings.

 

Does iReport Source, Inc. and/or third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Data collected in the Application forms, including text, audio, photos, and geolocation information is NEVER sent to our servers or to any third party unless the user mails that data specifically to those entities as part of the submission process. All of the data collected is saved only in the local storage of the mobile application and all previously collected data is removed from the device when a new report is started.

However, we may disclose any available User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

ꞏ as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;

ꞏ when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;

ꞏ if iReportSource, Inc. is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of this information, as well as any choices you may have regarding this information.

 

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

 

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We will retain Automatically Collected information indefinately and may store it in aggregate. User Provided Information is never stored on our servers.

 

Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at privacy@ireportsource.com.

 

Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of the information you collect. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Application. Please be aware that, although we endeavor provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.

User Provided Information is stored on the mobile device for one report to allow the user to continue entering data into an existing report if such data entry is interrupted. This data is removed from the local storage of the mobile application when the next report is started.

It should be noted that on the Android platform, the mobile application will store the data collected and the PDF report on simulated or actual “external” storage (such as a SIM card) so that the mail programs on the device are able to see that data for the purposes of attaching them to email message. On the iOS platform, the data is saved in file structure that is “Sandboxed” and only accessible to the Application.

The Application stores the data from the application in a non-encrypted manner so that it may be viewed by recipients without the need for decryption software or keys.

The Application uses the email program(s) on the mobile device to transmit the report and data collected at the end of the collection process. Once the data is attached to the email program, the Application has no way to ensure that the data is transmitted securely, to the right recipients, or that the recipients handle the received data in a secure manner. It is up to the user to ensure that their email clients are properly configured and that data is sent to the correct, responsible parties. Additionally, email clients may often save email messages they have sent within the mail application and it is the responsiblity of the user to remove those saved messages if they choose.

 

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here and informing you via email or text message. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

 

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. “Processing,” using or touching information in any way, including, but not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information, all of which activities will take place in the United States. If you reside outside the United States your information will be transferred, processed and stored there under United States privacy standards.

 

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at sales@ireportsource.com.
